William Miles Nathan is a painter and interior designer based in Paris and The Adirondack Mountains of New York State. He began his artistic career with a B.S. in Mathematical Economics and $1bn of technology mergers & acquisitions investment banking in San Francisco. Following finance, William joined BuzzFeed's tech team as a front-end web developer and was a co-founder of an interior design startup called Homepolish during their early days. His work has been featured in The New York Times, Elle Decor, Nylon, and InStyle – most recently for Object Hotel and artist residency which he founded and designed in Bisbee, Arizona.

In 2022, he launched WILLIAM PINX., a creative design studio. hi@williampinx.com
