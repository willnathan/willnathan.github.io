Will Nathan graduated from Wake Forest University with a B.S. in Mathematical Economics and began his career as an investment banker working on technology mergers & acquisitions in San Francisco. He left to join BuzzFeed as a front-end web developer in 2012. Following BuzzFeed, he co-founded an interior design startup called Homepolish as well as the vintage consignment app Object Limited. Will is a co-founder of the early-stage consumer investment fund Trail Mix Ventures and launched a non-profit devoted to digital art called The Current. Today you can find him in Los Angeles or at the hotel and artist residency he staked in Bisbee, Arizona.
